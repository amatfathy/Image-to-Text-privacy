Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-05-01

This extension, Image to Text, accesses only images that you explicitly paste into its popup (via Ctrl+V). Those images are sent solely to the OCR.Space API for text recognition. No images or extracted text are stored by this extension or shared with any third parties beyond OCR.Space.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information, location data, or browsing history. We do not sell or transfer your data for any purpose other than the single purpose of extracting text from images that you provide.

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at amatfathy99@gmail.com.
